Protein Pancakes

Description

If muscle could talk it would beg you to try this tasty breakfast treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana

  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp baking powder

  • coconut oil,
  • Nut butter, maple syrup and berries or sliced banana to serve
